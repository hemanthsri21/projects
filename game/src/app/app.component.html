Number Guessing Game

Objective: The goal of the game is to guess a sequence of 4 random numbers correctly. The numbers can range from 0 to 9, and your task is to figure out both the value and position of each digit.
'+' means that the guessed number and its position are both correct.
'-' indicates that the guessed number is correct, but its position is wrong.
'*' implies that the guessed number is incorrect and does not exist in the sequence.
